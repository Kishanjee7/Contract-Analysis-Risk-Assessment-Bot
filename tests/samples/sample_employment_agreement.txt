# Sample Employment Agreement for Testing

## EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2024, by and between:

**ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.**, a company registered under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at 123 Business Park, Mumbai 400001 (hereinafter referred to as the "Employer" or "Company")

AND

**Mr. Rahul Sharma**, residing at 456 Residential Complex, Thane 400601 (hereinafter referred to as the "Employee")

WHEREAS the Employer desires to employ the Employee, and the Employee desires to accept such employment on the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

### 1. POSITION AND DUTIES

1.1 The Employee shall be employed as Senior Software Developer and shall perform such duties as are customarily associated with such position.

1.2 The Employee shall report to the Engineering Manager and shall follow all reasonable directions given by the Employer.

1.3 The Employee shall devote full time and attention to the business of the Employer and shall not engage in any other employment without prior written consent.

### 2. COMPENSATION

2.1 The Employee shall receive a monthly salary of Rs. 1,50,000 (Rupees One Lakh Fifty Thousand only), payable on or before the 7th day of each month.

2.2 The salary shall be subject to deductions for income tax, provident fund, and other statutory deductions.

2.3 The Employee shall be eligible for an annual performance bonus of up to 20% of annual salary, at the sole discretion of the Company.

### 3. WORKING HOURS

3.1 Normal working hours shall be from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

3.2 The Employee may be required to work additional hours as necessary to fulfill job responsibilities without additional compensation.

### 4. PROBATION PERIOD

4.1 The Employee shall be on probation for a period of 6 months from the date of joining.

4.2 During probation, employment may be terminated by either party with 7 days notice or payment in lieu thereof.

### 5. LEAVE ENTITLEMENT

5.1 The Employee shall be entitled to 15 days of paid annual leave per year.

5.2 Unused leave cannot be carried forward and shall lapse at the end of each calendar year.

5.3 The Employee shall be entitled to 10 days of sick leave per year, subject to production of medical certificate for leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.

### 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 The Employee shall maintain strict confidentiality regarding all proprietary information, trade secrets, customer lists, business strategies, and any other confidential information of the Company.

6.2 This obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of 5 years.

6.3 Any breach of this clause shall result in immediate termination and the Company may seek damages without limitation.

### 7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

7.1 All work product, inventions, developments, and intellectual property created by the Employee during employment shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Company.

7.2 The Employee hereby assigns and transfers all rights, title, and interest in such intellectual property to the Company.

### 8. NON-COMPETE

8.1 During employment and for a period of 2 years after termination, the Employee shall not directly or indirectly engage in any business that competes with the Company anywhere in India.

8.2 The Employee shall not solicit any customers or employees of the Company for a period of 2 years after termination.

### 9. TERMINATION

9.1 After completion of probation, either party may terminate this Agreement by providing 90 days written notice or payment in lieu thereof.

9.2 The Company may terminate employment immediately without notice for cause, including but not limited to misconduct, breach of confidentiality, or poor performance.

9.3 Upon termination, the Employee shall immediately return all Company property and shall not retain any copies of confidential information.

### 10. INDEMNIFICATION

10.1 The Employee shall indemnify and hold the Company harmless against all claims, losses, damages, and expenses arising from any breach of this Agreement or any negligent act.

### 11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

11.1 Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Mumbai.

11.2 Before initiating any legal proceedings, the parties shall attempt to resolve disputes through good faith negotiation for a period of 30 days.

### 12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

12.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations and understandings.

12.2 No modification of this Agreement shall be valid unless in writing and signed by both parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
